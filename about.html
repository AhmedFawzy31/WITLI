Mission

Walk in the Light International is committed to transforming Burkina Faso through sustainable community development. To see Burkina Faso break free from physical, mental, social and spiritual poverty; empowering people to follow Jesus and make disciples, who transform West Africa.

The Big Goal

The big goal:God willing, be in partnership with one community in every province in Burkina Faso by 2045.
